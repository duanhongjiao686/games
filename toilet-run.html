Game Description

Toilet Run is a hilarious and addictive endless runner game that puts a unique spin on the genre. Control a toilet on wheels as you navigate through various obstacles and challenges in this quirky adventure.

Key features include:

  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Multiple power-ups and collectibles
  • Increasing difficulty as you progress
  • Global leaderboard system
  • Fun and quirky visuals
  • Addictive gameplay loop

Jump over obstacles, collect coins, and try to reach the highest score possible. With its unique premise and engaging gameplay, Toilet Run offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.
